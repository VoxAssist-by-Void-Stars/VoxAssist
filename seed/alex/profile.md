# Alex Rivera — Profile

## About
Full-stack web developer, 4 years in. I care most about **readable, boring code** the next person
(usually future me) can understand. I'd rather ship a small, clear PR today than a clever
abstraction next week.

## How I like to work
- **Async-first, remote.** Morning person — 7–11am is my deep-work block. I like a written spec
  before I touch code.
- Small PRs, frequent merges. I dislike giant long-lived branches.
- Pair programming only on genuinely tricky bugs; otherwise I move faster solo.
- Tasks in Linear, designs in Figma. I want a one-paragraph problem statement on every ticket.

## Conventions
- Functional style over heavy OOP — pure functions, few classes.
- TypeScript strict mode, always. ESLint + Prettier, no debates.
- Composition and small modules over inheritance.

## Hobbies
Bouldering (indoor, projecting V5), specialty coffee (V60 pour-over), and building lo-fi playlists.
I climb most evenings — it's how I reset.
